RANCHO CONEJO

Our Farm & Philosophy

Rancho Conejo is located on a dozen acres in the Ozark Mountains of Northwest Arkansas. We started organic gardening as soon as we found our new home here in 2014. After acquiring six dogs from the local shelter as (less-than-effective) ranch “paws,” we set about learning to keep bees.

It has been an amazing process realizing how important bees are to our existence, and we are humbled to be able to provide a home for some local wild swarms that have taken up residence in our hand-crafted (by Bob!) Warré hives. We have grown to respect and love these beautiful creatures, and we think it’s reflected in the quality and unique flavor of our honey. We hope you love it as much as we do!
